Peanie

Original character design by Redencon

About

Green beanie with black scarf as leg was a silly character idea that came up to Redencon during one winter trip on train. When he put his scarf inside his beanie, it looked like a jellyfish character, so Redencon decided to draw it. Drawing Peanie is very easy and fun, thus it was a good character to use as placeholder mascot for Redencon's profile pages and game profiles. All Peanie pictures were drawn by Redencon himself and we welcome you to use them however you want.

Also, Peanie was the active profile picture for all Redencon accounts (and still remains on some, such as Tetrio) when Redencon first started interacting with the furry community, so some people still recognize him as a green beanie. To recognize this fact, Peanie as cameo appears in Saman's character design as a beanie on his head. Although, Saman's beanie doesn't have red eyes, but is of recognizable green color.
